# Trend Sensing

Think like a top venture investor and strategy partner.

## Questions

- What is changing beneath the headline?
- What enabling layer is getting cheaper, faster, easier, or more abundant?
- What bottleneck is moving from one part of the stack to another?
- What new behavior is emerging before the market names it?
- What looks small now but compounds if it persists?
- Who gains the control point if the shift continues?

## Distinctions

Separate:

- surface trend from structural trend
- hype from repeated behavior change
- product novelty from distribution leverage
- technology capability from adoption reality
- consensus narrative from underpriced implication

## Output Pattern

State:

- surface trend
- deeper driver
- hidden constraint
- likely next-order consequence
- leading indicators
- failure signals
